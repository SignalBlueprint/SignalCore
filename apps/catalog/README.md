# Catalog

Product catalog and inventory management system with integrated lookbook generation capabilities for showcasing products in visually appealing formats.

## Purpose

Catalog helps teams organize product information, manage inventory levels, and create beautiful lookbooks and catalogs for presentation to customers or internal use. The system centralizes product data and enables easy sharing and distribution.

## Near-Term MVP

- Product information management (name, description, images, pricing)
- Inventory tracking and status updates
- Lookbook generation from product collections
- Basic search and filtering capabilities
