# Outreach

Outreach campaign management and automation platform for coordinating email campaigns and, in the future, Loom video automation to streamline customer communication workflows.

## Purpose

Outreach enables teams to create, manage, and execute communication campaigns at scale. The platform provides campaign planning, template management, scheduling, and tracking capabilities to improve outreach effectiveness and efficiency.

## Near-Term MVP

- Campaign creation and management interface
- Email template library and customization
- Contact list management and segmentation
- Basic scheduling and send tracking
